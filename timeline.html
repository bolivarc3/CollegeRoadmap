Timeline Tips

  • Start early - The most successful college applications begin in junior year
  • Prioritize tasks - Focus on the highlighted activities first
  • Be flexible - This timeline is a guide; adjust based on your specific situation
  • Track deadlines - Create calendar reminders for important deadlines
  • Balance activities - Don't take on too many tasks at once
