Senior Year To-Do List

  • Finalize your college list
  • Write and revise your essays
  • Request letters of recommendation early
  • Submit college applications by deadline
  • Fill out the FAFSA and explore aid options
  • Compare offers and visit admitted campuses
  • Make a decision and commit

Application Types

  • Early Action (non-binding)
  • Early Decision (binding)
  • Regular Decision
  • Rolling Admission

Post-Acceptance Prep

  • Plan for housing and meal plans
  • Attend admitted student events
  • Purchase supplies, tech, and dorm essentials
  • Plan your schedule, orientation, and move-in

Mental Health & Time Management

Use planners and stay organized. Reach out to campus resources for mental wellness, tutoring, and support groups as needed.
