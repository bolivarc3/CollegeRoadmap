Top Scholarship Platforms

  • Fastweb: Over $1.5 million in scholarships, free to use
  • Scholly: Filters scholarships based on personal criteria (Paid)
  • Cappex: Offers scholarships and college-matching tools
  • Chegg: Known for textbooks and scholarship search (Free)
  • Niche: College reviews + scholarships
  • Scholarships.com: Over $19 billion in aid, free and comprehensive
  • ScholarshipOwl: Premium matching service for students
  • Unigo: Internships, reviews, loans, and scholarships

Steps to Maximize Your Chances

  • Create accounts and fill out all profile information
  • Use filters to narrow down relevant opportunities
  • Track due dates in a calendar
  • Set reminders for recurring scholarship applications
  • Keep copies of essays and reuse/modify for new applications

Final Tips

  • Don’t assume you’re unqualified — apply anyway
  • Check email often for updates
  • Have an accountability partner
  • Make it a weekly routine to apply
