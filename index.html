Junior Year Roadmap

  • Study for the ACT/SAT
  • Take the ACT/SAT
  • College Search
  • Explore Career Interests
  • Networking & Professional Engagement
  • Apply for Scholarships

Senior Year Roadmap

  • Finalize College Choices
  • Write and Submit Applications
  • Complete FAFSA and Financial Aid Forms
  • Visit Campuses and Make Final Decision
  • Prepare for College Life

Application Process

Understanding application deadlines and requirements is key. Ensure you gather necessary documents, such as transcripts and letters of recommendation.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Research scholarships early and apply to as many as possible. Fill out the FAFSA as soon as it opens to maximize financial aid opportunities.

College Life Preparation

Start developing time management and budgeting skills to prepare for the transition to college life.
