What is a Passion Project?

A project that aligns with your personal interests and values. Great for college apps and personal growth.

Steps to Start

  • Pick a topic you're excited about
  • Set clear, achievable goals
  • Plan your timeline and tasks
  • Document your journey
  • Reflect and evaluate progress

SMART Goals

  • Specific: Define exactly what you want to achieve
  • Measurable: Track progress with clear metrics
  • Attainable: Make sure it’s realistic
  • Relevant: Align with your personal values
  • Time-Bound: Set a deadline

Project Tools

  • Risk Assessment: Identify risks and mitigation plans
  • Gap Analysis: Reflect on what’s missing or can be improved
  • Task Table: Assign tasks with roles and descriptions
  • Milestones: Group major phases of your project
