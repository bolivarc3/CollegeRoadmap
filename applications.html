Letters of Recommendation

Request at least 2 months in advance. Ask in person if possible and follow up a week before the deadline.

  • Prepare a brag sheet to help your teacher write a strong letter
  • Follow school-specific submission procedures
  • Sample Reminder: “Dear [Recommender], just a gentle reminder the deadline is [Date]. Let me know if you need anything!”

FAFSA & Financial Aid

  • File the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) as early as possible
  • Explore CSS Profile for additional aid options
  • Research local/state-based grants and institutional scholarships

Important Tips

  • Track application deadlines using a calendar
  • Double-check submission details and follow-up communications
  • Stay organized with copies of all documents
