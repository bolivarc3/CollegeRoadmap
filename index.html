The College Roadmap

Our comprehensive guide takes students through four key phases of college preparation:

Phase 1

Self-Discovery (Junior Year Start)

Identify your interests, strengths, values, and personality through self-assessment tools and feedback.

Phase 2

Research Careers

Explore career clusters, investigate specific jobs, and conduct informational interviews.

Phase 3

Narrow and Evaluate Options

List potential majors and careers, evaluate education needs, costs, job demand, and start passion projects.

Phase 4

Focus and Apply (End of Junior - Senior Year)

Follow our monthly timeline to make final decisions and apply to colleges.
