Research Tools

  • Google Scholar: Search for peer-reviewed articles
  • JSTOR, EBSCOhost: Access academic databases
  • Occupational Outlook Handbook: Career data
  • LinkedIn: Network and conduct informational interviews

Plagiarism Prevention

  • Always cite your sources properly
  • Use quotation marks for direct quotes
  • Paraphrase carefully and still cite
  • Keep a running list of references
  • Use tools like Grammarly or Turnitin for checking

Effective Studying

  • Break content into small sessions
  • Use flashcards and active recall
  • Teach others or explain out loud
  • Use checklists and spaced repetition
  • Form or join a study group
