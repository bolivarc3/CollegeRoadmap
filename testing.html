ACT Breakdown

  • English – 75 Qs, 45 min
  • Math – 60 Qs, 60 min
  • Reading – 40 Qs, 35 min
  • Science – 40 Qs, 35 min
  • Optional Essay

SAT Breakdown

  • Reading & Writing – 64 min across 2 modules
  • Math – 70 min across 2 modules
  • Includes multiple-choice and grid-in responses

Study Methods

Self-Study

  • Flexible and cost-effective
  • Use past tests and prep books

Paid Tutoring

  • Accountability and structure
  • Immediate feedback and support

Tips

  • ACT is faster paced but generally easier questions
  • SAT emphasizes algebra, problem solving, and reading comprehension
  • Take full-length practice exams for stamina and strategy
